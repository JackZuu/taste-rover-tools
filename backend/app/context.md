# TasteRover Business Context

**SmarTR by TasteRover** is a food-truck operations tool for the UK market.

## What TasteRover does
- Operates a fleet of food trucks (vans) across UK regions
- Serves street food at events, markets, festivals, business parks, and high-footfall locations
- Menu changes daily based on weather, trends, seasonal ingredients, local demand, and upcoming events
- Price range: £3–£12 per item, average ~£7
- Serves mains (burgers, wraps, skewers, pizza), sides, snacks, desserts, hot drinks, and cold drinks

## Equipment available
- Each van has a mix of: kettle, broiler, fryer, grill, fridge, freezer, blender, coffee machine, microwave, panini press
- Not every van has every piece of equipment — menus must be feasible with available kit

## Key constraints
- Items must be servable from a food truck (no fine dining, no complex plating)
- Prep time under 5 minutes per item
- Portions are single-serve, grab-and-go
- Must cater to dietary needs: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free options expected
- UK audience — British tastes, seasonal UK ingredients, UK pricing in GBP

## Menu categories
- **Grill**: burgers, wraps, skewers, pizza
- **Sides**: fries, salads, rice
- **Snacks**: pasta, sandwiches, bites
- **Desserts**: ice cream, cake, fruit, pastries
- **Cold Drinks**: soft drinks, juices, iced coffee
- **Hot Drinks**: coffee, tea, hot chocolate
